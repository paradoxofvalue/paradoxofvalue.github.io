Pyro

Pyro is new digital cryptocurrency based on improved Dash blockchain. Established on a Proof of Work and Masternode system, that means everyone can participate in Pyro growing, to make more decentrilized and lack of Monopoly. Pyro self-reliant open source currency, that has undisputed benefits and will be used in our and comunity future projects. Pyro has integrated Difficulty Retargeting Using Dark Gravity Wave, transaction Anonymity Using PrivateSend, Masternodes that help keep integrity and stability of network.

Solutions

Our first solution in the Pyro ecosystem will be the crowdfunding platform. Crowdfunding is a method of collective financing based on voluntary contributions. Our platform will provide an opportunity for people who want to bring to life their idea into reality and find investors. No credit cards, banking systems, just pay with Pyro!
